❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I play offline?

Yes! All game modes (Classic, Daily Challenge, Time Challenge, and Stage Mode) work completely offline. Leaderboards require internet connection.

How do I earn more coins?

You can earn coins by:
• Completing puzzles
• Watching reward ads
• Maintaining Daily Challenge streaks
• Purchasing from the Store

How does Game Center work?

Your scores are automatically submitted to Game Center leaderboards. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in iOS Settings to compete with friends and players worldwide.

The app crashes / won't start

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen
2. Restart your device
3. Check for iOS updates
4. Reinstall the app (progress may be lost)

🎮 Game Tips

How to solve puzzles faster?

• Start with the top row, then work your way down
• Use the "Preview" feature to see the completed image
• Practice in Classic mode before attempting Time Challenge

What are stars for?

Stars indicate your performance in each level. Earn 5 stars by completing puzzles quickly with minimal moves in Stage Mode.
