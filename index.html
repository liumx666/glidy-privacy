Glidy Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Glidy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Game progress, high scores, and in-game statistics
  • Advertising ID: For personalized advertising (can be disabled in device settings)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our game services
  • Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Tracking game progress and achievements via Apple Game Center
  • Analyzing game performance and user engagement

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

For more information, see: Google Privacy Policy

Apple Game Center

We use Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center uses your Apple ID to store your scores and progress. You can manage Game Center settings in your device settings.

For more information, see: Apple Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not store personal data on external servers unless you use Game Center, which is managed by Apple.

Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can delete your game data by uninstalling the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings
  • Disable Game Center integration
  • Delete your game data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
